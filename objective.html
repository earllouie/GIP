Objective

  • To provide young workers, particulary the poor/indigent and young workers opportunity to demonstrate their talents and skills in the fiels of public service with the ultiamte objective of attracting the best and brightest who want to pursue a career in government service, particulary in the fields and disciplines related to labor and employment; and

  • To fast track the implementation and monitoring of DOLE programs and projects related to employment facilitation and social protection, all of which diretcly contributes to the goal of inclusives growth through massive employment generation and substantial poverty reduction.
